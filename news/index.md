- Adaptable Radial Axes (ARA) mapping functions:
  - `ara_unconstrained_L2()`
  - `ara_unconstrained_L1()`
  - `ara_unconstrained_Linf()`

  - `ara_exact_L1()`
  - `ara_exact_Linf()`
  - `ara_ordered_L2()`
  - `ara_ordered_L1()`
  - `ara_ordered_Linf()`  
      
- Plotting functions:
  - [`draw_ara_plot_2d_standardized()`](https://manuelrubio.github.io/aramappings/reference/draw_ara_plot_2d_standardized.md)  
      
- Vignettes:
